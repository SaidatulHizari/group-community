Things to Do in Setiu, Terengganu

Explore nature, beaches, culture, and unique attractions in Setiu.

Visit Setiu Wetlands

Discover Malaysia’s largest wetland ecosystem with mangroves, lagoons, and rich wildlife.

Relax at Pantai Penarik

A peaceful beach famous for golden sands, fishing villages, and fresh coconut drinks.

Enjoy Setiu River Cruise

Take a scenic boat ride through wetlands, spotting birds and enjoying sunset views.

Stay at Terrapuri Heritage Village

Experience traditional Malay architecture and heritage culture in this unique resort.

Try Local Seafood

Setiu is well-known for fresh seafood like grilled fish, prawns, and keropok lekor.

Visit Turtle Hatchery

Learn about turtle conservation efforts and witness baby turtles being protected.
