Nature & Local Environment

Explore the natural beauty and environment of Setiu.

Rich Biodiversity

Setiu is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna. Its rivers, mangroves, and forests provide habitats for many species, including rare birds and aquatic life. Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect these natural resources for future generations.

Local Environment & Activities

The local environment of Setiu supports traditional livelihoods such as fishing, farming, and eco-tourism activities. Visitors can enjoy scenic rivers, wetlands, and serene landscapes while experiencing the community's sustainable way of life.
