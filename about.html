About the Setiu Community

Discover the traditions, culture, and lifestyle of Setiu, Terengganu.

Traditional Practices

Setiu has a rich history of traditional practices, from weaving to handcrafts. These skills are preserved by the locals and passed down through generations.

Learn More

Nature, Flora & Fauna within the Setiu Community

Nature plays an important role in shaping life in the Setiu community. The presence of diverse flora and fauna in mangroves, wetlands, and coastal areas supports both the ecosystem and the community’s way of life. This close relationship highlights the importance of environmental awareness and conservation among local residents.

Fishermen & Coastal Life

The fishermen of Setiu play an important role in the community, preserving sustainable fishing practices and providing fresh seafood to locals and visitors alike.
